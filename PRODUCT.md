# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Local business owners and educated professionals (Doctors, Clinics, Architects, Interior Designers, Event Planners, Wedding Vendors, Real Estate Agents, Brokers, Travel Agents) who understand the need for a digital presence but need an expert partner to build it. They are busy, value quality, and need a solution that enhances their efficiency and user base.

## Product Purpose
Planicle is a premium software agency that builds websites, apps, and (eventually) AI workflows. The purpose of this landing page is to immediately instill confidence, showcase technical excellence, and convince high-end local professionals to book a deal. It needs a "wow factor" that separates Planicle from cheap generic agencies.

## Brand Personality
Premium, confident, precise.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS templates, cheap "Fiverr-style" agency pages, overly playful or cartoonish aesthetics, cluttered or messy layouts.

## Design Principles
- **Show, don't tell:** Use the interface itself (the "wow factor" hero) to prove technical competence.
- **Expert confidence:** Keep copy concise and layout precise. Let the work breathe.
- **Premium restraint:** Avoid generic startup tropes; use high-quality typography and a sleek, high-contrast dark aesthetic that feels expensive.
- **Exceptional Motion:** Implement a simple, smoothly and minimally animated style. Use buttery-smooth microinteractions that scream quality without being excessive. Balance is key—rely on exponential ease-out curves and subtle shifts rather than bouncy or hyperactive movement.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Modern web standards (WCAG 2.1 AA baseline). Semantic HTML, proper contrast, and considerate motion.
